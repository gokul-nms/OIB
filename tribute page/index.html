EDUCATION

EDUCATION

Sundar Pichai was born in Tamil Nadu's Madurai city. His father, Regunatha Pichai, worked as an electrical engineer for the British corporation GEC, and his mother, Lakshmi, was a stenographer. Additionally, his father owned a facility that made electrical components. Pichai attended Jawahar Vidyalaya Senior Secondary School in Ashok Nagar, Chennai, and the Vana Vani School at the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras for his schooling. He graduated from IIT Kharagpur with a degree in metallurgical engineering. He received his M.S. in materials science and engineering from Stanford University and his MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, where he was recognized as a Siebel Scholar and a Palmer Scholar, respectively.

Career

Sundar Pichai's Career

He started his career as an engineer and product manager at a semiconductor maker (Applied materials) in Silicon Valley, USA. Sundar completed MBA from Wharton in 2002 and joined McKinsey & Company as a consultant. Finally, he joined Google in 2004. He was in charge of Google Drive and managed the product management and innovation activities for a number of Google's client software products, and also created other programmes, including Gmail and Google Maps. Sundar Pichai convinced the co-founders of Google, Sergey Brin, and Larry Page to launch Google’s browser. Sundar played an important role in the final launching of Google Chrome, in 2008.God help those who help themselves, and the launch of Google Chrome brings phenomenal success to the Sundar Pichai. Chrome became the No. 1 browser in the world, surpassing competitors such as Firefox and Internet Explorer. Sundar Pichai became an internationally famous figure following the thrilling success of the Google Chrome. Finally after the gap of 11 years, since he joined Google; he was selected as the next CEO of Google on August 10, 2015. The Alphabet Inc. was formed in 2015 as a Google’s parent company. Sundar Pichai was awarded 273,328 shares of Google's holding company Alphabet in Feb.2016. Finally, on December 3, 2019, he became the CEO of Alphabet Inc.

Achievements

His Achievements

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive who is currently the CEO of Alphabet Inc., the parent company of Google. Some of his notable achievements are:
Google Chrome: Pichai played a key role in the development of Google Chrome, which is now the world's most popular web browser. He also oversaw the launch of Chrome OS, a Linux-based operating system designed for use with web applications.
Android: Pichai led the development of Android, the world's most popular mobile operating system. Under his leadership, Android became the dominant mobile platform, with over 2 billion active users worldwide.
Google Drive: Pichai was responsible for the creation of Google Drive, a cloud-based storage and collaboration service that has over 1 billion users.
Google Apps: Pichai was instrumental in the development of Google Apps, which includes Gmail, Google Docs, Google Sheets, and other productivity tools. These apps have transformed the way people work and collaborate online.
Google Assistant: Pichai led the development of Google Assistant, an AI-powered virtual assistant that is now available on over 1 billion devices worldwide.
Artificial Intelligence: Pichai has been a strong advocate for artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning. He has helped to drive Google's efforts in AI research, and has also pushed for the responsible development and use of these technologies.
Alphabet Inc.: Pichai was named CEO of Alphabet Inc. in 2019, making him one of the most powerful figures in the tech industry. Under his leadership, Alphabet has continued to innovate and expand its offerings in a wide range of areas, from healthcare to autonomous vehicles.
